Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2025

Aura: Shared Sky & Star Diary ("the app") respects the privacy of its users. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Data We Collect

The app collects the following data to provide its functionality:

  • Account information: Email address when signing in with Google or Apple (optional account linking)
  • Device identifier: A randomly generated device ID for anonymous usage
  • Notification token: Firebase Cloud Messaging token for sending push notifications
  • User content: Stars you create (name, color, date, position, notes)
  • Shared sky data: Shared sky membership information

How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing core app functionality (star creation, shared skies)
  • Sending push notifications
  • Enabling data persistence through account linking

Your data is never used for advertising, profiling, or sold to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and notification services. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: Optional account linking. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In: Optional account linking. Apple Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. To delete your account or request data removal, please contact us at the address below.

Data Security

Your data is stored encrypted within Firebase infrastructure. All data transmission is protected via HTTPS/TLS protocol.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from this age group.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
